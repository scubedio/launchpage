Why We Exist

The word serendipity became known from The Three Princes of Serendip - a Persian fairy tail about adventures of three princes who made series of accidental discoveries in their quest of a lost camel. What do you have in common with the three princes of serendip?
Louis Pasteur, French microbiologist, chemist, and innovator, famously stated, “Chance favors the prepared mind.” At S3 we will help you prepare for new opportunities that will lead to successful outcomes. S3 helps people in organizations to re-envision and re-invent their authentic best. We co-create with our partners the right conditions for them to succeed and thrive. Our Services

We listen and learn to understand. We guide and nudge to support.
Our approach is leveraging a wide range of tools and in developing solutions grounded in the following principles:
  • We shine a light on shadows that hinder organizational progress.
  • We act as a mirror to help you transform these shadows into transformative action.
  • We help co-create spaces for learning and unlock opportunities for growth.

Team Whole

Is your team whole? Is your team’s collective intelligence and output greater than the sum of its individuals? Team Whole is an approach that allows your team to re-imagine collaboration by envisioning the team’s true goal, identifying and respecting each individual’s perspective and strengths and aligning them in the pursuit of that goal.
